About Us



In the early of 2021, Iman Studio was originally called as Meister Art. Meister Art was created by Muhammad Najmi Aiman Bin Muhammad Hamzan as a form of part-time hobby and passsion and also as a source of income. With a knowledge of photography, the journey of becoming a freelance photographer were started. After a few months of experience, Our Managing Director sees the potential and a bright future in the industry.

Because of the support and encourangement together with his passion in the industry, Iman Studio Enterprise was born and registered with SSM in 19th May 2021 and officially entered the photography industry. Previously Iman Studio only focus on photography service only, then slowly expended to videography serivce. Later joined by Mohamad Aidil Daniel Bin Mohamad Ali as a co-owner / partner in Iman Studio.

On the beginning, Iman Studio were only operated by two people to run the business but now we have growing team of five. Even though there's only five people, each one of the member hold a crucial role to make it a success such as marketing, accountant, sales team and the photographer and the videographer itself.

On 15th february 2022, after almost a year of dedication and hardwork we finally managed to open our new physical HQ at 98 Floor A Jalan Batu Unjur 1, Bayu Perdana, 41200 Klang, Selangor Darul Ehsan.

We specialize in wedding photography, family or personal potrait, corperate event, and etc. We often travel to client location even it take us couple of hours to reach just to capture the perfect moment in the perfect place. We will be there in every step to make sure those precious moment are captured all the time. Till now, we have more than a thousands portfolio and have been dealing with hundreds of client.
